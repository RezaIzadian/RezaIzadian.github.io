01 · About

Turning complex data into decisions

I'm a Senior Information Technology Consultant specializing in Microsoft .NET, Power BI, and enterprise business intelligence. Over nearly three decades I've led full software-development lifecycle initiatives, from requirements and architecture through development, modernization, deployment, and production support.

My focus has been mission-critical financial, reporting, and analytics platforms for federal organizations including Health Canada, the Public Health Agency of Canada, Department of National Defence, Elections Canada, Department of Justice, Fisheries and Oceans, and the Canadian Food Inspection Agency.

I lead modernization efforts: migrating legacy reporting environments to Power BI, designing semantic data models and ETL pipelines, and building secure, high-availability solutions that meet Government of Canada standards.
